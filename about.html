A Bit About This Project

People often pay taxes without knowing where their money goes and what it is being used for. This situation generally occurs because there is a lack of transparency for spending records, inaccessibility of resources and tools, inability to process massive data sets, inability to understand financial information, etc. Our group decided to create a platform for Prince Georgeâ€™s County citizens to have a better understanding of how the government is spending their taxes and what infrastructures they are being put into.



What We Hope To Achieve

We hope to provide an easily accessible and understandable resource (in the form of charts) for Prince George's County citizens to see where their tax money is going in terms of the total amount spent and what infrastructures they are being put into for the fiscal year 2019. This will allow them to see if the government is funding the agencies/infrastructures that they promised to, as well as areas that may need more funding for the next fiscal year.
